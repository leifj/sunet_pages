Client Startup Delay Across Mixed Subnets

Where a client computer and Luna SA are on different networks, any application (for example, our multitoken utility, or your client application program, etc) that is started on the client computer takes 20 seconds (the NTLS network timeout) to start up.  Once running, the application operates normally.  On Luna SA, an error is logged.

When both Luna SA and client are on the same subnet, the connection occurs without delay.
