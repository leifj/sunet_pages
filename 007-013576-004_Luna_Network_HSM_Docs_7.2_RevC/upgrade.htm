Updates and Upgrades

Gemalto releases periodic updates to the SafeNet Luna Network HSM appliance software and the HSM firmware, as well as updated versions of the SafeNet Luna HSM Client software. If you have recently purchased a new SafeNet Luna Network HSM and your organization requires FIPS certification, you can download and install a FIPS-validated version of the HSM firmware. You can download these updates as they become available from the Gemalto Customer Support Portal: https://supportportal.gemalto.com.

Depending on the model of SafeNet Luna Network HSM you selected at time of purchase, you may also be able to purchase upgrades to the HSM's capabilities, or increase the number of partitions you can create. These upgrades are provided through the Gemalto Licensing Portal (GLP).
